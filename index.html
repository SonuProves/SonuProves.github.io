About

"Passionate about olympiad mathematics and mentoring students."

★ still true, every day

I'm currently pursuing a B.Math at the Indian Statistical Institute, Bangalore, after completing a B.Sc. in Mathematics at Godda College. My interests sit at the intersection of real analysis, linear algebra, and number theory — and the slower, more stubborn kind of problem-solving that olympiad mathematics demands.

Alongside coursework, I've spent the last few years teaching — first as a private tutor for Classes 6–10, then as a Subject Matter Expert for JEE aspirants at Infinity Learn and Physics Wallah. I like explaining things clearly, and I like it even more when a student's confusion turns into a "wait, I get it now."

I'm fluent in English and Hindi, and can follow Bengali. When I'm not in a lecture hall, I'm usually working through an old olympiad set, or writing about wherever math happens to intersect with physics, computing, or economics — see my writing.
